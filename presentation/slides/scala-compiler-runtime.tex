\begin{frame}{Scala compiler}

  \begin{block}{}
    A compiler takes \textbf{code} and produces \textbf{machine code} (or \textbf{bytecode} in the context of the JVM).
  \end{block}

  \vfill

  \pause

  \begin{block}{}
    When the Scala compiler encounters a macro, it \textbf{compiles} it, \textbf{executes} the compiled bytecode, and treats the output as an \textbf{AST}.
  \end{block}
